Wake Up Animation Instructions

Frames 1-7 should hold for 0.2 seconds and Frame 8 should hold for 0.5 seconds.
The pattern of the frames should be:

1	2	3	4	5	6	7	8	7	6	5 	(instead of repeating, use First Frame of Idle animation or follow straight into the Idle animation sequence).

For Audio, The sequence lasts approximately 3 seconds.
